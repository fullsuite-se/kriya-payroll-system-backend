Kriya's Payroll System: Backend

This backend powers the payroll system within the Kriya HR ecosystem. It serves as the authentication provider for the HRIS app and ensures secure, reliable handling of payroll operations.

The system manages regular, special, and final pay runs for employees of Fullsuite, while also supporting multiple organizations through a multi-tenant architecture.

Additionally, the payroll backend provides the necessary data for BIR Form 2314 filing for each employee, ensuring compliance with reporting requirements.
